Bloom Sky

Bloom Sky exemplifies my ability to translate creative concepts into functional and engaging game systems. This 3D, infinite voxel-based strategy-puzzle game is about exploring the properties of seeds and growing them into platforms to reach the stars. It presented significant challenges in performance optimization and system design. I successfully addressed these challenges by implementing a robust chunk-loading system including optimizations for graphics. I developed a plant growth system using simple properties to grow complex, recursive structures, demonstrating my ability to design and implement complex systems with careful consideration of performance and player experience. To add time pressure, a mold corruption system efficiently tracks the spread of corruption across loaded and unloaded chunks using a modified breadth-first search. The result is a huge world shaped by simple, emergent rules and a puzzle for the player to solve.

This game was created with artwork by Khoa Tang.
