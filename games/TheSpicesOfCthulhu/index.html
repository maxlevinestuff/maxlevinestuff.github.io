The Spices of Cthulhu

You are an aspiring chef who has decided to collect the spices of the multiverse for their ultimate dish. However, harvesting the spice will cause a universe big crunch for each universe with matter removed from, and Cthulhu will relentlessly chase you. Once you collect three different spices from three worlds, an arrow will point your way home. Each universe has different physics. Avoid the black holes you create on your reckless cooking-fest. Fly into a universe to enter the level.

Controls:

WASD or Arrow keys - Steer ship

WASD or Arrow keys - Move platformer character and jump

Space - Reverse gravity (where applicable)
