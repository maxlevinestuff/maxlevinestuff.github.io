Yin Fang is a heavily internally divided werewolf who was cast into the lowest circle of the inferno - Treachery - for disobeying himself. Fight through the nine circles of hell to earn redemption, and perhaps bond the two conflicted halves along the way.

Theatrum Inferni was created by Max Levine, Andrew Campbell, Cameron East, and Logan Bodenheimer for Dr. Julio Baham√≥n's Introduction to Game Design and Development class at UNC-Charlotte.

In the realm where shadows intertwine, a Werewolf named Yin Fang,
An inner battle, a cosmic pang, in him, conflicting currents sang.

A noble soul, within him lies, yet an uncontrollable tide,
His other side, a feral guide, in the cosmic dance does bide.

For disobedience to self, he plummeted, to the abyss profound,
The lowest circle, Treachery's round, where redemption is rarely found.

Nine circles await, trials profound, each a cosmic test,
Tarot cards, his only bequest, in this infernal quest.

Yin Fang, in the dark abyss, must confront his inner strife,
A symphony of cosmic life, through nine circles, his journey's rife.

Yet within his Lupin form, salvation howls through the air,
Through trials profound, a savior rare, in the cosmic tapestry's solemn glare.
