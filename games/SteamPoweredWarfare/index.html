This is a 3D, steampunk-inspired, third-person tower-defense game. In this game, the Smithy uses toy turrets to defend the clock tower from corrupted, mechanical toys.

Steam Powered Warfare was created by Max Levine, Andrew Campbell, Joshua Froeschner, and Mack Larson for Prof. Daniel Jugan's Advanced Game Design & Development class at UNC-Charlotte.

  • Max Levine: Programming, Design
  • Andrew Campbell: Art, 3D Modeling, Animation, Design
  • Joshua Froeschner: Programming, Design
  • Mack Larson: Programming, Design
